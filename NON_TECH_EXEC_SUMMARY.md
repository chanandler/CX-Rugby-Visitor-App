# Non-Technical Executive Summary: Cemex Rugby Cement Plant Visitor App

## Overview
This iPad app is a digital front-desk system for managing site visitors at Cemex Rugby Cement Plant. It replaces manual sign-in processes with a faster, safer, and more reliable workflow.

## Why It Matters
- Improves reception efficiency by guiding a clear visitor sign-in/sign-out process.
- Strengthens site safety with real-time visibility of who is on site.
- Supports compliance and reporting through exportable records.
- Reduces operational risk through automated backups and protective access controls.

## How The App Is Used Day-To-Day
1. A visitor arrives and reception registers them.
2. The visitor is recorded as active on site.
3. When leaving, the visitor is checked out using a simple search and confirm step.
4. Reception can review current or historical records at any time.
5. Data can be exported when reporting is required.

## Key Capabilities
- Visitor registration with required visit details and optional vehicle registration.
- Check-out flow for departures.
- Sign In Book with views for active, archived, and all records.
- Fire alarm roll-call screen to account for visitors quickly during emergencies.
- CSV export and import for reporting, restore, and continuity.
- Automatic weekday cleanup of prior-day active records.
- Daily backups with retention cleanup and manual backup option.

## Security And Control
- Sensitive operational areas are PIN-protected:
- Sign In Book
- Fire Roll Call
- Settings
- The app auto-relocks after 5 minutes of inactivity or when returning from background after 5+ minutes.
- Settings are intentionally separate behind a dedicated cog access point.

## Reliability And Continuity
- Visitor data is stored locally in the app for operational use.
- Automatic backups reduce risk of data loss.
- Import/restore tools include preview and error handling to reduce bad data intake.

## Outcome
The app provides a practical, low-friction visitor process that improves safety readiness, reporting confidence, and front-desk consistency for the site.

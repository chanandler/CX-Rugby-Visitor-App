# Non-Technical Executive Summary: Cemex Rugby Cement Plant Visitor App

## Overview
This iPad app is the digital front-desk system for managing visitors at Cemex Rugby Cement Plant. It replaces paper sign-in with a clear, secure, and repeatable process.

## What It Delivers
- Faster visitor check-in and check-out for reception.
- Live visibility of who is currently on site.
- Better emergency readiness through a dedicated fire roll-call screen.
- Easier compliance/reporting with exportable records.
- Lower operational risk through automated housekeeping and backups.

## Daily Use Flow
1. Reception registers the visitor on arrival.
2. Visitor is recorded as active on site.
3. Visitor is checked out when leaving.
4. Reception can review active or historical records at any time.
5. Records can be exported or imported as needed.

## Key Functional Areas
- Visitor registration with required details and optional car registration.
- "I'm Leaving" search-and-confirm departure flow.
- Sign In Book with Active, Archived, and All views.
- Fire Roll Call with individual and "confirm all out" actions.
- Settings area for operational controls, backups, and app information.
- Analytics dashboard showing trends by day, week, month, or year.

## Security and Control
- Sign In Book, Fire Roll Call, and Settings are PIN-protected.
- The app automatically relocks after 5 minutes of inactivity.
- It also relocks after being in the background for 5+ minutes.
- Settings are accessed separately through a dedicated cog button.

## Continuity and Reliability
- Visitor records are stored locally in the app.
- Automatic weekday cleanup handles previous-day active records.
- Daily backups are created automatically with retention cleanup.
- Manual backup/export and import preview safeguards are included.

## Outcome
The app provides a safer and more reliable visitor process, with stronger accountability, easier reporting, and better front-desk consistency for site operations.
